About Me

I am a thirty one year old from rural Minnesota. I grew up on a farm where we raised and milked Dairy, then eventually switching to beef cattle and horses. When I graduated from SDSU with a bachelors in Marketing. I then landed a got a job at a media, merchandising, and marketing firm in south west Minneapolis. I worked there for eight years. My work there came to an end due to an accident that had happened on the job. Ultimately I ended up blowing out two discs in my back and herniated a third. Now after two separate spine surgeries, and countless spine injections. My back is doing somewhat better, and now I am pursuing a Computer Science biased career.
